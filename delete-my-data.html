Data Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your data associated with SponsoMate, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to advyay@advyay.com
  2. Use the subject line: “Request for Data Deletion”
  3. Include your Instagram username or the email used for login.
  4. Our team will process your request within 7 business days.

We take data privacy seriously and ensure complete deletion of your user data from our servers.
